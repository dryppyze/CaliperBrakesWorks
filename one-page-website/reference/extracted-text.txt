What does "per axle" mean? What does "from $99 per axle" include?
One axle means one pair of brakes. Front axle means both front wheels. Rear axle means both rear wheels. Starting install-only labour for standard non-German cars when you supply correct pads, rotors, hardware,
and sensors if needed.

What brake signs matter? Do I need pads only or rotors too?
Squealing, grinding, vibration, pulling, soft pedal, longer stopping, or a brake warning light should be checked. It depends on rotor condition. Grinding, vibration, deep grooves, rust, or thin rotors usually move the job
toward pads plus rotors.
Do both sides get replaced together? How long does brake service take?
Yes. Brake work is quoted by axle. Left and right sides on the same axle stay matched for even braking. Most clean brake jobs are planned by axle. Rust, wrong parts, seized hardware, or added work increases
appointment time.
